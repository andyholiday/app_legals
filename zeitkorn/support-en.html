Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?
All data stays exclusively on your device. Zeitkorn sends nothing to any server and requires no account. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How do I add a milestone?
Tap the plus button on the timeline, choose a category and a title, optionally add a photo and a note, then save.

How do I export my data?
Open Settings and tap “Export data”. You get a ZIP archive containing all babies, milestones, and photos.

How do I delete all my data?
Settings → “Delete account” permanently removes all personal data and photos from your device. This action cannot be undone.
